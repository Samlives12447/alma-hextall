The Cosy Woods

Deep in the green, leafy woods lived Goldilocks, brave Simba the Lion, and the kind Three Bears — Papa Bear, Mama Bear, and little Baby Bear.

They all lived together in a cosy cottage and were the best of friends!

The Hot Hot Desert

Far away in the hot, hot desert lived the Big Bad Wolf and a grumpy Bear.

They were always hungry and always grumpy. One day they sniffed the air...

"Hmm... porridge!"
Oh No! Here They Come!

The Wolf and the desert Bear started running through the night towards the cosy cottage in the woods!

THUD THUD THUD!

Inside, Baby Bear peeked out the window. "Someone's coming!"

Simba & Goldilocks Are Brave!

Simba stood tall in front of the cottage door and let out the biggest, loudest ROAR!

ROOOOAAAAR!!

Goldilocks spread her arms wide and said, "You are NOT coming in! This is our HOME!"

Oh No! They're Scared!

The Big Bad Wolf's ears went flat. The grumpy Bear's eyes went WIDE!

They turned around and ran ALL the way back to the desert as fast as their legs could carry them!

ZOOM! Gone!
Hooray! Everyone is Safe!

Everyone cheered! Papa Bear, Mama Bear, and little Baby Bear hugged Goldilocks and Simba tight.

"You were SO brave!" said Mama Bear. And they all had a big bowl of porridge together.

The End!
