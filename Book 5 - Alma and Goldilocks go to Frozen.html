Welcome to Arendelle!

One magical morning, Alma and Goldilocks stepped through a shimmering doorway and found themselves in the snowy kingdom of Arendelle!

Elsa and her sister Anna were waiting to greet them with the biggest smiles! "Welcome!" said Elsa, sending a burst of sparkling ice into the air.

Ooooooh! Ahhhhhh!

Page 1

Ice Skating and Ice Creams!

Elsa made a beautiful ice rink appear in a flash! All four friends laced up their skates and glided across the shimmering ice.

Then Anna handed out the most delicious ice creams! Strawberry for Anna, icy-blue for Elsa, vanilla for Goldilocks, and a chocolate one almost as big as Alma herself!

Wheeeee! Yum! Yum! YUM!

Page 2

Oh No! Jafar the Wicked Wizard!

Suddenly the sky turned dark and stormy! Jafar the Wicked Wizard appeared with his snake staff raised high!

He pointed his staff and a blast of scorching heat shot out! Every single ice cream melted into a sad little puddle on the ice.

"Mwah ha ha! No ice cream for anyone!"

Even tiny Alma's chocolate one. She looked at her empty cone and her lip wobbled.

Page 3

Elsa's Lesson!

Elsa knelt down beside little Alma and took her tiny hands gently. "Everyone has magic inside them," she whispered. "Close your eyes. Feel the cold in your heart. Now let it out!"

Alma closed her eyes. She thought of snowflakes. She thought of icy stars. And then... something extraordinary happened.

WHOOOOSH! Sparkle! Sparkle! Sparkle!

Blue light glowed from Alma's tiny little hands!

Page 4

Ice Cream for EVERYONE!

Ice cream cones erupted into the air — strawberry, chocolate, vanilla, mint, ice-blue, purple and more! One for everyone, and extras floating all around!

HOORAY FOR ALMA!

Elsa gasped. Anna leapt for joy. Goldilocks cheered so loud the snow fell off the mountains. Tiny Alma stood in the middle of it all, glowing with her new ice powers and the biggest smile in the whole kingdom!

Page 5

To Jail, Jafar!

Alma used her brand new ice powers to freeze Jafar's feet to the ground — WHOMP! — and Goldilocks quickly locked the jail door with a big golden key!

"Let me out!" Jafar grumbled from behind the bars, his wonky hat sliding down his head. But nobody was listening — everyone was too busy eating their ice creams!

HOORAY for ALMA and GOLDILOCKS!

Arendelle was safe. The ice creams were delicious. And tiny Alma had the most magical adventure — and the most magical powers — of anyone in the whole wide world.

The End!
