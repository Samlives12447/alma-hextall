🌲 The Camping Trip! 🌲

Alma, Goldilocks and Simba arrive in the big forest for their very first camping adventure! They put up their tent under the twinkling stars. "Tonight we make the best campfire ever!" shouts Alma, bouncing with excitement.

1

😟 Oh no! No fire... 😟

Alma tries the matches. Scritch! Scratch! Nothing at all. Goldilocks rubs two sticks together. Still nothing! Simba shrugs his big lion shoulders. No fire means no hot dinner — and no marshmallows! The forest night grows cold and very dark.

2

⚡ Charmander is here! ⚡

Suddenly, a Poké Ball rolls out of Alma's bag! BOOM! It bursts wide open! A bright orange glow explodes into the dark night. It's Charmander — Alma's very own Pokémon! "Char! Char!" it cries happily, bouncing on its little orange feet.

3

🔥 The Campfire is lit! 🔥

Charmander takes a huge deep breath and… WHOOOSH! A powerful blast of orange fire shoots out! The logs catch and roar to life instantly. The campfire blazes bright and warm. Everyone cheers! "Thank you, Charmander, you are amazing!"

4

❄ The Monster has Goldilocks! ❄

ROOAAR! A gigantic ice monster crashes through the trees — the Monster from Frozen! He has grabbed Goldilocks and frozen her solid inside a thick block of ice! "Alma, help!" cries Goldilocks from inside the ice.

5

🔥 Use Flamethrower, Charmander! 🔥

"Charmander, use Flamethrower!" shouts Alma. Charmander takes the biggest breath of his life and blasts the mightiest fire he has ever made! WHOOOOOOSH! The monster's snow and ice melts away everywhere. The monster wobbles… and falls with a mighty crash!

6

🎉 Goldilocks is Free! 🎉

The magic ice melts away and Goldilocks is free! She runs to her friends, laughing and shouting with joy. Charmander leaps and roars happily. Alma hugs everyone as tight as she can. "You are the best Pokémon in the whole world, Charmander!"

7

The End!
🔥❤🌲

Alma, Goldilocks, Simba and Charmander — best friends forever!
