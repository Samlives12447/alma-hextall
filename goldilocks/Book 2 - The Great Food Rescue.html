🌳 Chapter 1: The Happy Woods of Fairyland 🌳

In the magical Woods of Fairyland, everyone was happy! The fairy folk had a wonderful market full of bread, fruit, pies, and big pots of porridge. 🍞🍎🥧

Goldilocks, Simba, and Abu the cheeky monkey loved living here with all their friends.

😈 Chapter 2: Three Sneaky Villains 😈

In a dark cave in the desert lived three sneaky villains: Scar the wicked lion 🦁, the Big Bad Wolf 🐺, and a grumpy Bear 🐻.

Scar smiled his nastiest smile. "We'll sneak into the woods and steal every crumb of their food!"

Mwahaha! 😈
😱 Chapter 3: The Food is GONE! 😱

In the middle of the night, Scar, the Wolf, and the Bear crept into Fairyland… and stole every single bit of food! The bread, the fruit, the pies — ALL gone! 😭

The fairy folk woke up to empty stalls and cried big, sad tears.

Oh no! 😱
💪 Chapter 4: The Brave Heroes Set Out! 💪

Goldilocks stamped her foot. Simba shook his mane. Abu the monkey chattered and bounced up and down!

"We know where they've gone," said Simba. "The desert! Let's go get that food back and bring it home!" 🏜️

To the desert! 🌵
🌵 Chapter 5: Into the Desert! 🌵

Abu was small and quick — he scurried up the cave and spotted all the stolen sacks! 🐒

Simba let out a mighty ROAR that shook the desert. Goldilocks spread her arms wide and shouted, "Hand it ALL back!"

Scar's eyes went WIDE. The Wolf's tail went between his legs. The Bear whimpered! 😨

🎒 Chapter 6: The Big Carry Home! 🎒

Scar, the Wolf, and the Bear ran away screaming into the distance — so fast their paws left dust clouds! 💨

Simba carried the biggest sack. Goldilocks filled her basket to the brim. Abu bounced along on top of the pile, chattering with joy!

We got it ALL! 🎉
🎊 The End: Fairyland is Fed! 🎊

The fairy folk cheered and danced! Every stall was full again with bread, fruit, pies, and porridge. 🥳🍞🍎🥧

Simba, Goldilocks, and cheeky little Abu were the heroes of Fairyland FOREVER. And as for Scar, the Wolf, and the Bear…

…they were too scared to ever come back! 😄

The End! 🌈
