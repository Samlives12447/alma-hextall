A New Friend!

Deep in the cosy woods, Goldilocks had a wonderful surprise! Little Alma had come to visit — she was just three years old, with beautiful blond hair just like Goldilocks!

Simba wagged his tail. Baby Bear jumped up and down. "YAY! A new friend!" everyone cheered.

Page 1

Oh No! Baby Bear is Missing!

After lunch, everyone went outside to play. Then Mama Bear stopped and counted heads. Papa Bear. Mama Bear. Goldilocks. Alma. Simba.

"Wait!" gasped Mama Bear. "Where is Baby Bear?!"

Oh no! Oh no! Oh no!

Page 2

Searching the Woods!

They searched behind every tree. They looked under every bush. They called and called!

"BABY BEAR! BABY BEAR!"

Then tiny Alma stopped very still. She looked down with her sharp little eyes, and pointed her small finger at the ground.

"Look! Paw prints!"

Page 3

Alma is SO Brave!

The paw prints led to a BIG tangled bramble bush! Goldilocks pushed and pushed at the branches. "I'm too big!" she said sadly.

But tiny Alma spotted a small gap at the bottom. She took a big brave breath and smiled her biggest smile.

"I can do it!"

Page 4

Found!

Alma crept through the brambles and there — tucked inside a cosy hollow log — was Baby Bear! Fast asleep, snoring softly!

Snoooore... Snoooore...

Alma gently placed her tiny hand on his soft paw. "Wake up, Baby Bear," she whispered sweetly. "Everyone is looking for you!"

Page 5

Heroes Big and Small!

Baby Bear and little Alma came back through the brambles together. EVERYONE cheered!

HOORAY for AMLA!

Mama Bear scooped tiny Alma up and held her HIGH in the air! "You are our HERO!"

Goldilocks smiled her biggest smile. "Even the littlest helpers can be the biggest heroes!"

The End!
