Book 6

Alma, Charmander & the Camping Adventure!

Told by Alma Hextall

Page 1

Alma, Goldilocks, and the Three Bears were going camping in the big forest. Charmander trotted beside Alma, its Poké Ball swinging at her belt. "This will be the best adventure!" said Alma.

Page 2

When they set up camp, they needed a fire to cook dinner. Alma rubbed two sticks together. Goldilocks blew and blew. Mama Bear tried with two rocks. Nothing. Not a single spark!

Page 3

Suddenly Alma's Poké Ball burst open with a flash of light! Charmander leaped out, its little flame tail glowing in the dark. Alma's eyes went wide. "Charmander! Can you help us?"

Page 4

Charmander nodded and took a big breath. It blew a warm flame at the sticks — and WHOOOOSH! A great fire crackled and blazed into the night. "YAAAAY!" all the friends cheered.

Page 5

But suddenly a HUGE rumble shook the trees. Marshmallow, the snow monster from the Frozen forest, had stomped in and captured Goldilocks inside a block of ice! Alma's eyes went wide. "Goldilocks! NO!"

Page 6

“Charmander — use Flamethrower!” shouted Alma. Charmander drew in a huge breath and launched a blazing orange jet of fire straight at Marshmallow! The snow monster began to melt. The ice around Goldilocks cracked and split.

Page 7

CRACK! The ice burst open and Goldilocks leapt free! Marshmallow melted into a puddle and sank into the snow. Alma squeezed Charmander in a big hug. "You are the best Pokémon in the whole world!" she said.

The End!

Goodnight, brave little ones ✨
