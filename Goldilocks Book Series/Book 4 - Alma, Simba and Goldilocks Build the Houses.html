Let's Build the Houses!

The Three Little Pigs needed three new houses! Goldilocks, Simba and tiny Alma came to help — and so did their new friend Abu the cheeky little monkey!

They built a house of straw, a house of sticks and a house of shiny bricks. Even the littlest helpers did their best!

BANG! BANG! BANG!

Page 1

The Sneaky Villains!

But while the pigs went to play, something sneaky happened!

The Big Bad Wolf crept into the straw house. A mean Villain Bear snuck into the stick house. And sneaky Scar tiptoed into the brick house.

CLICK! CLICK! CLICK!

They locked every single door and laughed their horrible laughs!

Page 2

Locked Out!

The Three Little Pigs came home and found every single door LOCKED! Goldilocks pushed with all her might. Simba roared his biggest roar. But nothing worked!

Oh no! Oh no! Oh NO!

Then tiny Alma looked very carefully at the bottom of each house. She spotted something the others had missed — three tiny little gaps. "I see a way in!" she whispered to Abu.

Page 3

Alma and Abu Sneak In!

Under the light of the moon, Alma took a deep breath and crawled through the tiny gap at the bottom of the straw house.

And Abu the clever monkey squeezed down through the chimney of the stick house. Nobody else in the world could fit!

Shhhhh... tiptoe, tiptoe!

Goldilocks and Simba held their breath outside and waited...

Page 4

SNIP! SNIP! SNIP!

The Wolf, the Villain Bear and Scar were all fast, fast asleep — snoring in their big cosy chairs!

Snooore... Snooore... SNOOORE!

Tiny Alma crept up to the Wolf. Little Abu tiptoed up to the Bear. And between them, they gave every villain the most spectacular haircut the forest had ever seen!

SNIP! SNIP! SNIP! BUZZ! BUZZ!

Off came the Wolf's fluffy grey fur. Off came the Bear's shaggy dark hair. Off came Scar's magnificent mane!

Page 5

Run Home COLD!

The villains woke up, looked at each other, and SCREAMED!

WAAAAAAH!

The Wolf, the Villain Bear and Scar took one look in the mirror and ran straight out the doors — all the way home — shivering and cold with no hair to keep them warm!

The Three Little Pigs got their houses back. Goldilocks cheered. Simba roared with delight. And Alma and Abu got the biggest celebration hug of all!

HOORAY for ALMA and ABU!
The End!
